Free Spins Feature — The Heart of the Game

The Free Spins feature is the standout mechanic in Brick House Bonanza and the primary driver of its 10,000x max win potential. To trigger it, players need 6 or more Piggy Bank Scatter symbols to land anywhere on the reels during a base game spin. This awards 6 initial free spins, and before the round begins, all positions containing triggering Scatter symbols are marked with a straw square — laying the groundwork for the game's signature house-building mechanic.

During the free spins round, each new Scatter that lands marks its position on the reels. If a Scatter lands on a position already marked with a straw square, it upgrades to a wood square. Land another Scatter on a wood square, and it upgrades again — this time to a brick square, the most valuable material. Additionally, every combination of 3 or more Scatter symbols during the free games awards +1 extra spin, extending the bonus round and giving players more opportunities to fill the board with high-value brick positions.

  • Straw Houses (lowest tier): Reveal random money prizes of 0.6x, 1x, or 1.5x the bet. These form the base layer of every triggered free spins round and help accumulate smaller wins while the higher-value positions develop.
  • Wood Houses (mid tier): Upgrade the reward potential significantly, revealing prizes of 2x, 3x, 4x, or 6x the bet — plus access to the Mini Jackpot (12x), double Mini (24x), or Minor Jackpot (60x). A key milestone in building toward the biggest prizes.
  • Brick Houses (highest tier): The premium outcome, revealing prizes of 10x, 15x, 20x, 100x, or 250x the bet, alongside access to all four jackpots: Mini (12x), Minor (60x), Major (500x), and the Grand Jackpot worth 5,000x the stake — the single largest award in the game.
  • Full Board Bonus: If all 15 reel positions are marked with brick squares, no further upgrades occur. Instead, every subsequent Scatter landing on the board pays a fixed reward of 10x the bet — a powerful guaranteed payout mechanism for fully loaded boards.
  • End-of-Round Reveal: When all free spins are exhausted, the Wolf character appears and blows the houses down, revealing the hidden cash prizes beneath. All prizes are summed and paid as a single lump sum — creating a climactic and highly rewarding conclusion to each bonus round.

Bonus Wheel — Four Ways to Win Instantly

Separate from the Free Spins trigger, the Bonus Wheel activates when 3 or more Bonus symbols land simultaneously anywhere on the reels during a base game spin. This awards one spin on the bonus wheel, which can deliver one of four distinct prize outcomes. The Bonus Wheel is an independent feature, meaning it adds another layer of win potential on top of the base game and free spins mechanic — making every spin feel like it carries meaningful bonus potential.

  • Random Jackpot: Awards one of the four fixed jackpots directly — Mini (12x), Minor (60x), Major (500x), or Grand (5,000x) — without needing to enter the free spins round. The most direct path to the game's biggest prizes.
  • Mega Bank Feature: Awards a single special spin with oversized 2x2, 3x3, and even 5x3-sized Scatter symbols on the reels. The sheer size of these Mega Scatters makes it virtually guaranteed to trigger the free spins feature immediately, often with a heavily pre-loaded board.
  • Windmill Feature: The triggering Bonus symbols roll across the reels from right to left, marking and upgrading every reel position they pass through before launching the free spins round. This creates a heavily pre-marked board, dramatically boosting the value of the upcoming bonus round.
  • Brick House Feature: The triggering Bonus symbols convert their positions directly to the highest-value brick squares before launching free spins. Additionally, all Scatter symbols that land during the subsequent free games also mark their positions as brick — making this the most powerful wheel outcome for maximising the end-of-round payout.
